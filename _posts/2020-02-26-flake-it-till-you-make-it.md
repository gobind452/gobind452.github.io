Apart from machine learning, I also try (and usually fail) to optimize for breadth in my interests and endeavours- 

* I was a Teaching Assistant for the course Database Management Systems under Prof Maya Ramanath, and an academic mentor for Linear Algebra for first year students. Teaching is pretty fun.
* I was a victim of the crypto-currency FOMO in 2017-18, and tried my hand at cryptocurrency trading and investing in ICOs. Most of my returns were negative, with a few positives which kept my tiny portfolio afloat. And in some time, I and the hype exited the crypto trading space.
* I was a part of the Economics Club at IIT Delhi, first as an executive and then as a Creative Coordinator. Fun fact - I had zero knowledge of designing and creative work before being appointed as the head of creative vertical. Yet I was able to manage a team of executives and deliver (this might be the biggest accomplishment of my life). I still have zero knowledge of design.
* My course list in undergrad consists of courses in Robotics to General Relativity to Quantum Computing to Efficient Approximation of Kernel Functions (yes). I keep a detailed list of courses here.
* I tried my hand at playing drums while I was working at Citi.
